Split bills, skip the awkward math

Create an invoice, share it with your housemates, and the app sends everyone their share automatically. When they pay, you just approve it – simple, fair, done.

Chores don’t do themselves

Make a task, assign it to anyone in the house, and watch it get checked off when it’s done. No more confusion about “whose turn it is.”

All talk, no clutter

Keep every house update in one place so nothing slips through the cracks. Whether it’s rent reminders, chore updates, or just dropping memes, the chat makes sure everyone stays in the loop.

Lists everyone can agree on (mostly)

Need groceries, cleaning supplies, or just more snacks? Add it to a shared list that everyone in the house can see. Make as many lists as you want—grocery, yard, random Amazon things—and watch them update in real time. When it’s time to check out, the app turns your list into a bill so costs are split automatically.

This started out as a small personal project, just me tinkering around, but it’s slowly grown into something bigger than I expected. I built it because I know how messy living with roommates can get – bills, chores, lists, and those endless group chats – and I wanted something that actually makes it easier.

I’m committed to keeping this app alive and improving it as much as I can. Life will get busy (it always does), but I’ll keep showing up for this. If you have ideas, feedback, or just features you’d love to see, I’d honestly love to hear from you. You can reach me anytime at omobabaoke324@gmail.com. If I’m able to add your suggestion, I’ll let you know – this app should feel as useful to you as it does to me.

Thanks for giving it a shot and being part of the journey. This is just the beginning.
